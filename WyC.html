The 4 opening, 1 central, and 4 closing Deeds
are for the core commitments:
Gratitude, Humility, Faith, Charity, Hope.

These are the most important commitments,
so focus on these first.
◆ represents five earnings, so these Deeds
account for over half of your rewards.
Indeed, being clean accounts for over half of these rewards,
so be pure, be clean.


The remaining 12 Deeds are for the daily commitments.
Health, Truth, Love, Beauty, Peace.

At least twenty-five core earnings are needed
to obtain these daily earnings.
This boundary exists so that people
focus on core commitments first.
They may be interlaced with the core commitments,
as shown, or spoken afterwards.
