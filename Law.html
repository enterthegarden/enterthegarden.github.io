(Guard your histories with careful, but consistent reading.)

[Personal]
To all people be fair, but forgiving.
So, have consistent standards made known to others.
Listen calmly to both sides of a dispute.
And find a path, however hard, to forgive people.

[National]
Read the sources of law and find righteous rulings.
So, study the laws of nations having the most power.
Study the laws of nations bearing the most people.
And deeply study the laws of your own nation.

[International]
Live as nations, separate and self-governing.
So, keep the equality of sovereignty of every nation.
Keep trade between nations voluntary.
And keep intervention between nations limited.

Wish for the Greatest Patience to help those with less.
Be hopeful for the Justice & Mercy yet to come.
