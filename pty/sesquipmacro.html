Sesquipedalian Macropolysyllabification

Full Text

Quaquaversal lucubration about pervicacious torosity and diverticular prosiliency in diatonic formication and chromatic papulation, engendering carotic carmination and decubital nyctalopia, causing borborygmic susurration, terological urticulation, macroptic dysmimia, bregmatic obstipation, crassamental quisquilousness, hircinous olophonia and unflexanimous luxation, often produce volmerine cacumination and mitotic ramuliferousness leading to operculate onagerosity and testaceous favillousness, as well as faucal obsonation, parallelepipedal psellismus, pigritudinous mysophia, cimicidal conspurcation, mollitious deglutition and cephalotripsical staltitiousness, resulting despite Hesychastic omphaloskepsis, in epenetic opisthography, houstrophedonic malacology, lampadodromic evagination, chartulary cadastration, merognostic heautotimerousness, favaginous moliminosity, fatiscent operosity, temulencious libration and otological oscininity, aggravated by tardigrade inturgescence, nucamentacious oliguria, emunctory sternutation, veneficial pediculation, fremescent dyskinesia, hispidinous cynanthropy, torminal opitulation, crapulous vellication, hippuric rhinodynia, dypneic nimiety and favillous erethism, and culminating in opisthographic inconcinnity, scotophiliac lipothymia, banausic rhinorrhea, dehiscent fasciculation, oncological vomitrution, nevoid paludality, exomphalic invultation, mysophilic excrementatiousness, flagitious dysphoria, lipogrammatic bradygraphy, orectic aprosexia, parataxic parotexia, lucubicidal notation, permutational paranomasia, rhonchial fremitus, specular subsaltation, crapulous crepitation, ithyphallic acervation, procephalic dyscrasia, volitional volitation, piscine dermatology, proleptic pistology, verrucous alopecia, hendecaphonic combinatoriality, microaerophillic pandiculation and quasihemidemisemibreviate illation.


Section 1

Quaquaversal lucubration
about pervicacious torosity and diverticular prosiliency
in diatonic formication and chromatic papulation,
engendering carotic carmination and decubital nyctalopia,

● quaquaversal : going off on all directions from the center
● lucubration : intense and prolonged study or meditation; especially, late at night
● pervicacious : Extremely stubborn or headstrong; wilful, obstinate
● diverticular : relating to a small out-pouching of an organ wall such as the large intestine or urinary bladder
● prosiliency : the act of leaping forth or forward; projection
● diatonic : relating to or characteristic of a musical scale which contains seven pitches and a pattern of five whole tones and two semitones; particularly, of the major or natural minor scales
● formication : an abnormal skin sensation similar to that of insects crawling over or within the skin; a tactile hallucination involving such a sensation
● chromatic : characterized or caused by, or relating to, colour or hue papulation : the formation of papules
● engendering : bringing about
● carotic : of or relating to stupor
● carmination : charming, incantation
● decubital : pertaining to or resulting from decubitus (the manner or posture of lying in bed)
● nyctalopia : reduction or loss of vision in dim light; night blindness

Section 2

causing borborygmic susurration,
terological urticulation,
macroptic dysmimia,
bregmatic obstipation,
crassamental quisquilousness,
hircinous olophonia and unflexanimous luxation,

● borborygmic : Of, pertaining to, or resembling borborygmus; rumbling
● susurration : A low and indistinct whispering sound; a murmur
● terological : (?) relating to design
● urticulation : (?) a sensation of having been stung by nettles
● macroptic : relating to macropsia -- a condition of the eye in which objects appear to be unnaturally large
● dysmimia : an inability to use gestures effectively
● bregmatic : pertaining to the bregma -- the anatomical structure on the skull where the coronal suture and sagittal suture meet
● obstipation : severe constipation caused by intestinal obstruction
● crassamental : relating to the crassamentum -- the solid component of blood, blood from which serum has been removed; a mass of coagulated blood; a blood clot
● quisquilousness : miscellaneousness
● hircinous : relating to goats
● olophonia : (?)
● unflexanimous : not flexanimous -- having power to bend or influence the mind; moving, affecting
● luxation : the action of dislocating or putting out of joint; the condition of being dislocated; dislocation; an instance of this

Section 3

● volmerine : (?)
● cacumination : pointedness
● mitotic : relatingto mitosis -- a type of cell division
● ramnuliferousness : small-branchedness
● operculate : having an operculum -- a covering flap in animals, such as a gill cover
● onagerosity : (?)
● testaceous : having a shell which is not articulated
● favillousness : ashiness

parallelepipedal psellismus,
pigritudinous mysophia,
cimicidal conspurcation,
mollitious deglutition and cephalotripsical staltitiousness,

● faucal : relating to the fauces -- or, opening of the throat
● obsonation : (?)
● parallelepipedal : having the form of a parallelepiped
● psellismus : a defect of speech; esp. stammering.
● pigritudinous : lazy
● mysophia : (?)
● cimicidal : relating to Cimicidae -- wingless flat-bodied bloodsucking insects
● conspurcation : the act of defiling
● mollitious : sensual and self-indulgent
● deglutition : the action or process of swallowing
● cephalotripsical : relating to cephalotripsy -- the act or operation of crushing the head of a foetus in the womb in order to effect delivery
● staltitiousness : (?)

Section 5

resulting despite Hesychastic omphaloskepsis,
in epenetic opisthography, houstrophedonic malacology,
lampadodromic evagination, chartulary cadastration,
merognostic heautotimerousness,
favaginous moliminosity,
fatiscent operosity,
temulencious libration and otological oscininity,

● Hesychastic : soothing or calming
● omphaloskepsis : contemplation of one's navel as an aid to meditation
● epenetic : Bestowing praise; eulogistic; laudatory
● opisthography : A writing upon the back of anything, as upon the back of a sheet already written upon on one side
● houstrophedonic : (? - possibly 'boustrophedonic' was meant)
● malacology : the branch of zoology that deals with molluscs
● lampadodromic : relating to an Ancient Greek race run by young men carrying lighted torches
● evagination : unsheathing
● chartulary : a medieval manuscript register containing full or excerpted transcriptions of important documents, especially of originally loose, single-sheet charters
● cadastration : surveying for the purpose of showing boundary and property lines.
● merognostic : one who knows in part but not all
● heautotimerousness : (?) relating to fearfulness of the self
● favaginous : formed like or resembling a honeycomb in appearance; cellular.
● moliminosity : hugeness
● fatiscent : having cracks or chinks
● operosity : the state or quality of being industrious or busy
● temulencious : (? - possibly a variant of 'temulent' -- drunken)
● libration : The action of librating; motion like that of the beam of a balance oscillating upon its pivot; swaying to and fro.
● otological : relating to the branch of medicine concerning the ear and its diseases
● oscininity : (? possibly relating to 'oscinine' -- of songbirds)

nucamentacious oliguria,
emunctory sternutation, veneficial pediculation,
fremescent dyskinesia,
hispidinous cynanthropy,
torminal opitulation, crapulous vellication,
hippuric rhinodynia,
dypneic nimiety and favillous erethism,

● tardigrade : sluggish; moving slowly
● inturgescence : a swelling; the act of swelling, or state of being swelled
● nucamentacious : of a fruit: resembling a small nut, spec. indehiscent
● oliguria : reduction of urine output
● emunctory : pertaining to the elimination of waste from the body
● sternutation : sneezing
● veneficial : (? - possibly 'poisonous')
● pediculation : an infestation with crab lice
● fremescent : becoming murmurous; roaring
● dyskinesia : abnormality or impairment of voluntary movement
● hispidinous : (? - possibly covered with stiff hair or bristles)
● cynanthropy : a delusion in which a person believes he or she is transformed into a dog
● torminal : relating to severe pains in the stomach
● opitulation : help, aid, assistance
● crapulous : characterized by gross excess in drinking or eating; intemperate, debauched
● vellication : the action or process of pulling or twitching; irritation or stimulation by means of small or sharp points; titillation or tickling
● hippuric : a crystalline carboxylic acid formed in metabolism and present in urine, esp. that of horses and other herbivores
● rhinodynia : pain in the nose
● dypneic : (?)
● nimiety : excess, redundancy, superfluity; an instance of this
● favillous : consisting of or resembling ashes
● erethism : excitement of an organ or tissue in an unusual degree; also transferred morbid over-activity of the mental powers or passions

● inconcinnity : inelegance
● scotophiliac : (?)
● lipothymia : a condition or feeling of faintness
● banausic : not operating on an elevated level; mundane
● rhinorrhea : excessive mucous secretion from the nose

● fasciculation : a brief spontaneous contraction affecting a small number of muscle fibres, often causing a flicker of movement under the skin
● oncological : relating to the study and treatment of tumours

● invultation : the practice of sticking pins in a wax doll in order to cause pain or harm to a victim

● bradygraphy : (? possibly 'slow writing')

permutational paranomasia,
rhonchial fremitus,

● acervation : a heap, a mass

● volitional : pertaining or relating to the action of willing
● volitation : flying, flight
● piscine : of or relating to fishes
● dermatology : the study of the skin and its diseases
● proleptic : of a calendar, extrapolated to dates prior to its first adoption; of those used to adjust to or from the Julian calendar or Gregorian calendar

● verrucous : relating to warts

● hendecaphonic : (?)
● combinatoriality : (?)

● quasihemidemisemibreviate : (?)
