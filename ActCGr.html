Expressing Gratitude is a transcendent act.
The ultimate practice is being thankful in all acts.

The Resting Gratitude Deed has 3 styles:
Recital, Symmetrical, Universal


The Recital Style

The Recital Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).
Try your best to remember these sentences said upon rising (OGr):

The Symmetrical Style

The Symmetrical Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).

For each sentence, remember the specific (x) things in each area of life noticed upon rising [OGr].
One thing for each root area:
Health, Truth, Love, Beauty, Peace

Earnings Acts Earnings

+
  “I am thankful for (x), e.g. my eyes.”
“As with these I may have grown in my Health.” +

“As with these I may continue hoping to find Truth.” +
 

+
  “I am thankful for (x), e.g. my parents.”
“As with them I still have someone to Love.” +
 

+
  “I am thankful for (x), e.g. these flowers.”
“As with these I may continue hoping to find Beauty.” +
 

+
  “I am thankful for (x), e.g. my leader.”
“And with all these I can still hope for Peace.” +
 


The Universal Style

The Universal Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).

For each sentence, start remembering the things you were grateful for upon rising [ActOGr].

Remember those small things you would have otherwise missed.
Remember those large things you would have otherwise taken for granted.
And especially remember how much you could have been without.

It is so beautiful to continuously
find new things to be thankful for.
And to never forget what you are thankful for.
