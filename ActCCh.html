The Universal Style

The Universal Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).

For each sentence, start remembering the people you committed to help upon rising [OCh].

Remember distant people you would have otherwise missed.
Remember those people
who you would otherwise have taken for granted.
And especially remember how much you could have done for people.

It is so beautiful to continuously find
new people to be helpful to.
And to never forget who you can help.
