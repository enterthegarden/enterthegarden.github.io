◆ Who am I? ◆

I'm English, born in '89, and I teach maths & science for a living.

My thinking style is dominated by: lists, systems, words, numbers, etc.

◆ Why did I create the Garden? ◆

I created the Garden several years ago, and it has undergone several revisions during this time.

The whole goal of the Garden has consistently been to help people in self-education.

Its initial versions were a lot more 'spiritual', and far less practical, than the Garden you see today.

In fact, I had quite a wild imagination regarding how far it could go.

Nowdays, I'm far more down to earth about it.

Is it a system? An art piece? A metaphysical landscape?

Better off to just see it as a toolkit at first.

If you can make use of it and improve your own life, then it has served its purpose.
