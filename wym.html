There are 21 Daily Deeds, which give 125 Daily Earnings.

9 are the Core Deeds.
These reward 65 Daily Earnings called Core Earnings.
12 are the Root Deeds.
These reward 60 Daily Earnings called Root Earnings.

The 9 Core Deeds regard:
Gratitude, Humility, Faith, Hope, Charity

They are the:
4 opening Deeds for 25 earnings
1 central Deed for 15 earnings
4 closing Deeds for 25 earnings

So, these Deeds give over half of your Daily Earnings.
And being clean gives over half of your Core Earnings.
So, be clean, be pure.

The 12 Root Deeds regard:
Health, Truth, Love, Beauty, Peace

At least 25 Core Earnings are needed to obtain any Root Earnings.

It is your responsibility to measure these earnings well.
However, for each, a beginning way to measure is provided.
