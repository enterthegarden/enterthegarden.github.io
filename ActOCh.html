Giving Charity is a truly loving act.
Remembering those needing help is the beginning of Charity.

The Rising Charity Deed has 3 styles:
Recital, Symmetrical, Universal


The Recital Style

The Recital Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).
All that must be done is to say these sentences:

“And I must guide them to Peace.” +
 


The Symmetrical Style

The Symmetrical Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).

For each sentence, notice someone specific (x) in each area of life.
At least one person for each root area:
Health, Truth, Love, Beauty, Peace

Earnings Acts Earnings

+
  “I think of (x), e.g. those without food.”
“And I must help their Health if I find them.” +
 

+
  “I think of (x), e.g. those learning to read.”
“And I must help their Truth if I find them.” +
 

+
  “I think of (x), e.g. those stuck in disaster.”
“And I must connect
with Good Character if I find them.” +
 

+
  “I think of (x), e.g. those I could give a gift to.”
“And I must help their Beauty if I find them.” +
 

+
  “I think of (x), e.g. those following me.”
“And I must guide them to Peace.” +
 


The Universal Style

The Universal Style has 5 acts, which are sentences to say.
Each act gives 1 earning (+).

For each sentence, start noticing people to help by yourself.

Remember distant people you would otherwise miss.
Remember your people
who you would otherwise take for granted.
And especially remember how much you can do for people.

It is so beautiful to continuously find
new people to be helpful to.
And to never forget who you can help.
