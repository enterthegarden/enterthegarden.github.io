The Universal Style

Start noticing people to help by yourself.

Remember distant people you would otherwise miss.
Remember your people who you would otherwise take for granted.
And especially remember how much you can do for people.

It is so beautiful to continuously find new people to be helpful to.
And to never forget who you can help.
